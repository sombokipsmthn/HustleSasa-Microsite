ArtXChange

The project "ArtXchange: connecting creative youth in Africa and Europe" aims at improving the inclusion, empowerment and connection of young people in the creative sector in Africa (Kenya and Somalia) and Europe (Italy and Sweden), enhancing the potential of creative industries in promoting sustainable development, job creation, income opportunities and social inclusion. ArtXchange involves young creative professionals, youth organizations and civil society, promoting collaboration, capacity building and exchanges among target countries.
